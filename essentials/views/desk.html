Individual Approach to Education!

The basic principle of the individual approach is respecting learners. This means not making discrimination according to the race, nationality, sex, religion, political belief, respecting learners’ socioeconomic status, differences in their personalities, learning habits etc. School Esssentials is trying to develop a high degree of attention to individual differences and needs of learners. School Esssentials is small and has basic rules for learner behaviour and academic performance. Learners are aware that they can contact their tutor throughout the day, they are offered individual consultations, they are often told that educators believe in their ability to learn. There is no comparison between unsuccessful and successful learners, all the problems are fluently discussed with educators and learners, teachers and the tutor try to help underachievers, educators use active learning strategies.

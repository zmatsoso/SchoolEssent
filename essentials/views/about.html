About the Author

Zipho Matsoso

Zipho's background is, in a word, "eclectic".

His success in Computer Sciences (Skills of which are all self-taught, till recently where he needed to acquire some credentials for some complience issues) came after a successful career as a Para-Legal Assistant and a Financial Investment Advisor with Old Mutual SA.

Zipho established Computer Software Solutions (Now Galaxy Computers) in August of 1998, as a Programmer and Technical Support Rep.
He was performing a dual duty as Manager of Technical Support and Manager of Internal and External System Programming.
As the Manager of Technical support, he oversaw the phone support of products, as well as performed the duties of Systems Operator on the CIS Galaxy Computers Forum.
In the area of Internal and External Systems Programming, he was responsible for maintaining operating programs and developing new systems used by South African Institutions, which were all developed using Clarion products for applications and SQL; Access and Top-speed as back-end databases.

Zipho further established another company in September of 2002 - "MATSOSO INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS" (Matsoso IT Solutions). His new company catered for the Technological needs of schools in a revolutionary, proactive and innovative approach.

In June 2005, Zipho wrote his first Book - "Computer Applications Today", this book is a hands-on introduction to a personal computer, and is a useful learning tool for South African Learners and Educators.
